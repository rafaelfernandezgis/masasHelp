Modifying attributes

  1. In order to change its attributes, you will need to enable the Editing switch button first and then click on the area.



  1. Now, you can modify its geometry using the vertex handlers.



  1. Or click again inside the incident area and then the dialog attributes windows will show up.



  1. As you can see, there are some fields already populate by default, such as Emergency Type, Emergency Level and when the incident started (current date and time). You can change those values any time. So we are going to add some description, a location, who is the EOC Manager and the Incident Commander and people evacuees





  1. Now you will be able to see that those changes were already applied on the incident Area.
