Modifying geometry

  1. You will need to switch on the Editing button.
  2. And then, click on the area:



  1. You can use the vertex handlers to modify the geometry, or the middle handlers to add new vertex. You will notice the icon will move to the centroid of the area.


  1. Finally, if click on a vertex handler, that vertex will be to remove it.
